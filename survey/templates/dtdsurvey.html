Door to Door Survey

Through door to door survey ,we can assess the following points:

  • Name and mobile number of each family head
  • Assessing caste support in the area
  • Candidate's favour and opposition on the basis of area and family
  • Keeping the social media user in touch
  • Assessing the effective issue of the area according to the family

    • Benefits of survey:

  • The voter list becomes clear (through verification) from door to door survey through the electoral roll
  • Databases received from door to door surveys can be able to build a very strong hold (via Social Media, SMS, Voice Call) through IT Services
